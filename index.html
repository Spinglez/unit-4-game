Skull Guess Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four skulls below, by clicking on a skull you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each skull will be hidden until you click on it

Each time the game starts, the game will give you a new random number and change the value of each skull.
